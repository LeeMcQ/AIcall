About Sacred Wisdom

Sacred Wisdom runs Google's Gemma AI model entirely in your browser using Google's own MediaPipe inference engine — the same technology behind the Google AI Edge Gallery app.

📲 To install as an app:
In Chrome → tap ⋮ menu → Add to Home Screen. After one online visit, the app and model work fully offline.
ℹ️ How it works:
Models are downloaded once and cached in your browser's IndexedDB. Every subsequent load is instant and fully offline. All inference runs on your phone's GPU via WebGPU.

Recommended model by device:

  1. Any Android (4GB+ RAM) → Gemma 3 1B
  2. Budget / older phone → Gemma 3 270M
  3. Flagship (8GB+ RAM) → Gemma 3 4B

✦ All computation happens on your device ✦
