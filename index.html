About Sacred Wisdom

Sacred Wisdom runs an AI model entirely in your browser — no internet required after the first download.

📲 To install as an app:
In Chrome → tap ⋮ menu → Add to Home Screen.
The PWA will install like a native app. The AI model loads once and works offline.
⚠️ If the model fails at 99%:
Your phone ran out of GPU memory. Switch to a smaller model (Qwen 1.5B recommended). Or switch to WASM mode — slower but always works.

Two engine modes:

  1. WebGPU – Uses your phone's GPU. Fast (5–15 tok/s). Requires Chrome 121+ on Android.
  2. WASM – CPU-based, 2–4× slower but works on any modern browser.

For the best religious model on Android:

  1. Install PocketPal AI or SmolChat from the Play Store
  2. Download a GGUF from Reformed Bible Expert GGUF (Q4_K_M, ~6.5GB, flagship phones only)
  3. Load it in the app and chat offline!

✦ All computation happens on your device ✦
